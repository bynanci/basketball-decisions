# Product Terminology

Use these canonical terms across navigation, UI labels, docs, and workflow descriptions.

## Canonical terms

- **Player Value**: explainable local score used for decision-support. It is not a grade.
- **Confidence**: evidence support level for a score, summary, workflow output, or recommendation.
- **Evidence**: event/source references that support an output and keep it traceable.
- **Artifact Health**: freshness, missing, and stale status used for reliability checks.
- **Review Queue**: quality review tasks that require human follow-up.
- **Coach Report**: coach-facing summary/report output.
- **Player Home**: player-facing next-action view.
- **Practice Feedback**: execution notes and signals captured after practice work.
- **System Lab**: data/model/source governance tools.

## Avoided terms

Avoid these terms in user-facing copy unless they are needed for historical context in older docs:

- grade
- ranking
- truth
- AI score
- automatic recognition
- artifact as a user-facing term outside **System Lab** and **Artifact Health** contexts

## Notes

- Preserve route paths when normalizing labels.
- Do not rename backend models while applying terminology updates.
- Prefer “Evidence” and “Artifact Health” wording in warnings, dashboards, and workflows.
