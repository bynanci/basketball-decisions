# Court IQ Demo Script

Use this script for quick onboarding demos.

## 10-minute walkthrough

1. Open `/` and click **Load Sample Project**.
2. Jump to `/development-dashboard` for product-area navigation and artifact health rollup.
3. Open the sample **Project Workspace** and **Pipeline** pages.
4. Show **Tracking Review** as the quality-control checkpoint.
5. Open **Player Value** summary and one player detail page.
6. Open **Coach Reports**, then **Drills** and **Practice Plans**.
7. Open **Workflows** and **Review Queue** to show operator recovery paths.
8. Optionally open `/start` and `/training` for player-role onboarding.

## Talking points

- The sample project is deterministic and local; no external media dependency.
- Court IQ favors artifact visibility and explicit freshness over hidden automation.
- Coaches, analysts, and players use different routes over shared artifacts.
