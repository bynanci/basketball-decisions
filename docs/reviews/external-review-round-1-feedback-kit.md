# ER2 External Review Round 1 Feedback Capture Kit

## Purpose
This kit standardizes how external reviewers capture feedback during ER2 Round 1 so teams can compare findings consistently across roles, identify high-impact issues quickly, and translate observations into actionable product decisions.

## Scope and Usage Notes
- Use this document as a **template pack** during moderated or async review sessions.
- Select only the role-specific forms needed for the current review wave.
- Keep raw notes objective; save interpretation for synthesis sections.
- Record evidence (quotes, timestamps, screenshots, logs) whenever possible.
- Do **not** use this kit to implement product changes directly; route findings through normal prioritization and planning workflows.

## 2) Universal 1–5 Rating Scale
Use this scale across all role forms for consistency.

| Rating | Label | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Very Poor / Strongly Disagree | Major breakdown; unable to complete key tasks or confidence severely reduced. |
| 2 | Poor / Disagree | Significant friction; completion possible only with workarounds or support. |
| 3 | Fair / Neutral | Mixed experience; usable but notable issues limit efficiency or trust. |
| 4 | Good / Agree | Works well for most needs; minor issues do not block outcomes. |
| 5 | Excellent / Strongly Agree | Clear, efficient, trustworthy, and role-fit with no meaningful friction. |

**Scoring guidance**
- Prefer whole numbers only (1–5).
- Require a short rationale for any score of **1** or **2**.
- If a prompt is not applicable, mark **N/A** and explain why.

## 11) Prioritization Rubric
Score each finding to support triage.

### A. Scoring Dimensions (1–5)
| Dimension | 1 (Low) | 3 (Medium) | 5 (High) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Impact | Minor annoyance | Noticeable workflow slowdown | Blocks critical decisions/outcomes |
| Reach | Isolated reviewer | Affects one segment | Affects most reviewers/roles |
| Frequency | Rare edge case | Intermittent | Consistent/reproducible |
| Risk | Low downside if unresolved | Moderate trust/usability risk | High trust/compliance/business risk |
| Effort-to-validate | Complex/unknown | Moderate | Quick to validate |

### B. Priority Score
Use:

`Priority Score = (Impact × Reach × Frequency) + Risk + Effort-to-validate`

Interpretation bands (adjust as needed):
- **High Priority:** 70+
- **Medium Priority:** 35–69
- **Low Priority:** <35

## 12) Synthesis Table Template
Aggregate cross-role insights for decision review.

| Theme | Supporting Findings | Roles Affected | Evidence Strength | User/Business Impact Summary | Recommended Next Step | Owner | Target Milestone |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Example: Recommendation trust | F-003, F-011, F-019 | Coach, Player, PM | High / Medium / Low | Trust gaps reduce adoption in high-pressure decisions. | Validate explanation improvements in next review cycle. | | |

**Synthesis guidance**
- Cluster by problem theme, not by requested feature.
- Note contradictory signals between roles explicitly.
- Separate immediate mitigations from longer-term bets.
